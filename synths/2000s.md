# ‚≠ê **3. Virtual Analog Refinement**

VA was born in the 90s, but got **polished and perfected** in the 2000s.

Key instruments:

* **Access Virus C and TI (2002 / 2005)**
  The trance & industrial powerhouse.

* **Novation Supernova II (2000)**
  Giant polyphony + huge FX.

* **Korg MS2000 (2000)**
  Affordable, flexible, cool retro sound.

* **Alesis Ion (2003)**

  Elastic audio, time manipulation, COSM filters.

These synths defined electronica, trance, industrial, and early EDM.
